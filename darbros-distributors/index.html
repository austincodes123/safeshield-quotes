Switching Is Easy. We Handle Everything.
  • 1 Once you give us the go-ahead, we bind your new policy with The Hartford immediately. Coverage starts right away with no gap.
  • 2 We contact your current carrier and submit the cancellation request on your behalf. You do not need to call them or send anything.
  • 3 Your current carrier will issue a prorated refund for any unused premium directly back to you. We will confirm the cancellation is processed and follow up until it is complete.
  • 4 We send you your new ID cards and certificates of insurance the same day. If any third parties (lienholders, lessors, contract partners) need to be notified, we handle that too.
Bottom line: you say "yes," and we take care of the rest. No paperwork, no phone calls to your old carrier, no gaps in coverage.
