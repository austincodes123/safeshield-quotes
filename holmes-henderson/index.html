Why Higher Deductibles?

We typically recommend $1,000 deductibles on Comp and Collision. A higher deductible keeps your premium lower and discourages filing small claims. Small claims are what cause your rates to spike at renewal. The goal is to use insurance for what it's meant for: the big stuff.

Why Higher Liability?

Your GEICO limits of $25K/$50K are the state minimum. If you're ever in an at-fault accident, those limits can get used up fast, and anything above that comes out of your pocket. We doubled your liability to $50K/$100K and your premium is still lower.
